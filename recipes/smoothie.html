Vegan Morning Smoothie

Description

A great breakfast smoothie to pair with some toast and coffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • ⅓ cup frozen chopped spinach
  • ½ cup frozen mixed fruit
  • 1 tablespoon flax seed meal
  • ½ scoop vegan protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • ½ teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • water to cover

Steps

  1. Layer banana, spinach, mixed fruit, flax meal, protein powder, chia seeds, and matcha powder in a blender in the order listed; add enough water to cover. Cover blender and blend until smooth.
